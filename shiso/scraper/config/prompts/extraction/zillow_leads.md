## Zillow Rental Manager — Lead Extraction Tips

- Leads are typically found under "Leads" or "Inquiries" in the left sidebar.
- Each lead card usually shows the tenant name, property, and date.
- Click into a lead to see full contact details (email, phone, message).
- Check for "New", "Read", and "Archived" tabs — extract from all of them.
- Some leads may only show a first name until you open the detail view.
- If there's a "Load More" button at the bottom, click it before extracting.
