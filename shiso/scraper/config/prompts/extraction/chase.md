This is a Chase bank account dashboard. Extract all visible checking, savings,
credit cards, loans, lines of credit, and investment accounts.

Chase-specific tips:
- Accounts are grouped by type (checking/savings, credit cards, loans)
- Click into each credit card or loan detail page for credit limit, APR, and payment history
- "See all accounts" or account type tabs may reveal additional accounts

Promo APR:
- Look for "Intro APR", "0% Intro APR", "Promotional rate", or "0% for X months"
- Check for "Promotional balance transfer APR" or "Purchase APR" sections
- Find the promo end date ("until", "through", "ends")
- Note the "Go-to rate" or "Regular APR" that applies after promo ends
